Eligible Vehicles

An “Eligible Vehicle” is a personal passenger (non-commercial) vehicle:

of which you have lawful possession;
for which you have all required permissions to use in connection with the Services and any Carpool Trip;
which is insured as required by applicable Law, including in at least the minimum financial responsibility amounts of the state in which such vehicle is registered (collectively, the “Required Insurance”);
which is designed to transport no more than 8 persons (including the driver);
which has a gross vehicle weight of less than 10,000 pounds;
for which use of the vehicle in connection with the Services and any Carpool Trip will not violate the terms of the Required Insurance;
which is in good working condition with fully-functional seatbelts;
which is not connected to any trailer or similar towed object;
which is roadworthy (including having no visible body damage and no check-engine lights);
which is not licensed as a transportation network company or other “for-hire” or commercial vehicle; and
which is up to date with all vehicle maintenance and upkeep recommended by the vehicle’s owners’ manual, maintenance guide, or similar document;
Offering a Carpool Trip.

Carpool Trip Definitions.

“Arrival Time” means the anticipated time that the Eligible Vehicle will arrive at a Destination Point for a Carpool Trip as indicated by the Platform;

“Available Seat” means a passenger seat with a fully functioning seatbelt in an Eligible Vehicle which a Driver Member would like to make available for Rider Members to bid on with respect to a Carpool Trip;

“Carpool Trip” means an upcoming trip a Driver Member plans to take using an Eligible Vehicle and that such Driver Member would like to make available for carpooling by allowing Rider Members to bid on at least one Available Seat;

“Departure Time” means the time that a Carpool Trip will begin as indicated by the Platform;

“Pick-up Point” means the starting location of a Carpool Trip as indicated by the Platform; and

“Destination Point” means the ending destination of a Carpool Trip as indicated by the Platform.

Offering a Carpool Trip. If you are a Driver Member, you can use the Platform in order to offer Rider Members the opportunity to search for and bid on Available Seats for a Carpool Trip that you plan to take. In order to do so, you will use the Platform to Supply certain information about the type of Carpool Trip on which you are offering Available Seats for Rider Members to bid on including the Pick-up Point, Destination Point, number of Available Seats, Departure Time, and Arrival Time with respect to such Carpool Trip. After you Supply such information the Platform will take such information and “match” such Carpool Trip with Rider Members who may be interested in the opportunity to bid on an Available Seat for such Carpool Trip (each, a “Potential Rider,” and collectively, the “Potential Riders”). In order to Supply information about a Carpool Trip on the Platform, such Carpool Trip must have at least one Available Seat.
