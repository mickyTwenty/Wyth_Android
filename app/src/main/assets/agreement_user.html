  • Locating a Carpool Trip.
    • 4.1 Carpool Trip Definitions.

      a“Arrival Time” means the anticipated time that the Eligible Vehicle will arrive at a Destination Point for a Carpool Trip as indicated by the Platform;
      b “Available Seat” means a passenger seat with a fully functioning seatbelt in an Eligible Vehicle which a Driver Member would like to make available for Rider Members to bid on with respect to a Carpool Trip;
      c “Carpool Trip” means an upcoming trip a Driver Member plans to take using an Eligible Vehicle and that such Driver Member would like to make available for carpooling by allowing Rider Members to bid on at least one Available Seat;
      d “Departure Time” means the time that a Carpool Trip will begin as indicated by the Platform;
      e An “Eligible Vehicle” is a personal passenger (non-commercial) vehicle:
      • 1 of which a Driver Member has lawful possession;
      • 2 for which a Driver Member has all required permissions to use in connection with the Services and any Carpool Trip;
      • 3 which is insured as required by applicable Law, including in at least the minimum financial responsibility amounts of the state in which such vehicle is registered (collectively, the “Required Insurance”);
      • 4 which is designed to transport no more than 8 persons (including the driver);
      • 5 which has a gross vehicle weight of less than 10,000 pounds;
      • 6 for which use of the vehicle in connection with the Services and any Carpool Trip will not violate the terms of the Required Insurance;
      • 7 which is in good working condition with fully-functional seatbelts;
      • 8 which is not connected to any trailer or similar towed object;
      • 9 which is roadworthy (including having no visible body damage and no check-engine lights);
      • 10 which is not licensed as a transportation network company or other “for-hire” or commercial vehicle; and
      • 11 which is up to date with all vehicle maintenance and upkeep recommended by the vehicle’s owners’ manual, maintenance guide, or similar document;
      f “Pick-up Point” means the starting location of a Carpool Trip as indicated by the Platform; and
      g “Destination Point” means the ending destination of a Carpool Trip as indicated by the Platform.
    • 4.2 Searching for a Carpool Trip. If you are a Rider Member, you can use the Platform in order to locate and bid on an Available Seat for a Carpool Trip. In order to do so, you will use the Platform to Supply certain information about the type of Carpool Trip for which you searching including the Pick-Up Point, Destination Point, number of Available Seats remaining, Departure Time, and Arrival Time with respect to such Carpool Trip. After you Supply such information the Platform will take such information and “match” you with Carpool Trips with Available Seats on which you may be interested in bidding (each, a “Carpool Option,” and collectively, the “Carpool Options”).

  • Bidding on a Carpool Trip.
    • 5.1 Suggested Reimbursement Amounts. If you find a Carpool Trip for which you would like to bid on an Available Seat, you will use the Platform to select the amount of reimbursement (the “Bid”) per mile you are willing to offer for such Carpool Trip. The Platform will suggest a Bid for each Carpool Trip (each a, “Suggested Bid Amount”) based on factors including the Bids that the Driver Member offering such Carpool Trip has accepted in the past. You are not required to offer a Suggested Bid Amount as a Bid, and your Bid will not necessarily be accepted for a particular Carpool Trip if you offer a Suggested Bid Amount.
    • 5.2 Notification of Accepted Bid. If you submit a Bid for a Carpool Trip and it is accepted by the Driver Member offering such Carpool Trip, you will be notified of such acceptance by the Platform and such Carpool Trip will be considered a “Confirmed Trip” and the Bid you offered for such Confirmed Trip at the time of acceptance by the Driver Member for such Carpool Trip will be referred to as a “Confirmed Bid.” For the avoidance of doubt, a “Confirmed Trip” is a Carpool Trip that has at least one confirmed Rider Member passenger (due to such Rider Member having a Confirmed Bid) and thus is scheduled to operate.
    • 5.3 Significance of a Confirmed Bid. If you have a Confirmed Bid on a Confirmed Trip, you are confirmed as a passenger on such Confirmed Trip and the number of Available Seats for such Confirmed Trip will decrease by one. If you do NOT have a Confirmed Bid on a Confirmed Trip, you will NOT be a passenger on such trip.
